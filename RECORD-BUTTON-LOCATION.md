# How to Find Record Button in Xbox Game Bar

## If you see Audio widget:

### Option 1: Look for Record Button
1. **Close the Audio widget** (click X)
2. Look for a **"Capture"** widget or **Record button** at the top
3. Click the **Record** button (red circle icon)

### Option 2: Use Keyboard Shortcut
- **Press `Win + Alt + R`** to start recording directly (no need to find button!)
- Press `Win + Alt + R` again to stop recording

### Option 3: Open Capture Widget
1. Click **"Widget Menu"** (three horizontal lines icon) at the top
2. Select **"Capture"** widget
3. Click **Record** button

---

## Alternative: Use Windows Snipping Tool (Easier!)

### Steps:
1. **Press `Win + Shift + S`**
2. At the top, click **"Screen recording"** icon (video camera icon)
3. Select area to record (or full screen)
4. Click **Record** button
5. Click **Stop** when done
6. Video is saved automatically

---

## Quick Method (Keyboard Shortcut):

**Just press: `Win + Alt + R`** to start recording!

No need to find buttons - this starts recording immediately.

Press again to stop.

---

## What You'll See When Recording:

- A small recording indicator at the top of screen
- Timer showing recording duration
- Press `Win + Alt + R` again to stop

---

## Video Location After Recording:

- **Xbox Game Bar:** `C:\Users\YourName\Videos\Captures`
- **Snipping Tool:** `C:\Users\YourName\Videos\Captures` or Downloads folder
