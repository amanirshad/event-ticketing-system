# How to Find Your Recorded Video

## Method 1: File Explorer (Easiest)

### Steps:
1. **Press `Win + E`** to open File Explorer
2. Go to: **`C:\Users\YourName\Videos\Captures`**
   - Replace `YourName` with your Windows username (e.g., `Akanksha`)
3. Your video will be there with a name like: `Recording_2025-11-08_15-30-45.mp4`

### Quick Path:
```
C:\Users\Akanksha\Videos\Captures
```

---

## Method 2: From Xbox Game Bar

### Steps:
1. **Press `Win + G`** to open Xbox Game Bar
2. Click **"Gallery"** icon (looks like photos/images)
3. You'll see all your recordings there
4. Click on a video to play it
5. Right-click → **"Open file location"** to find where it's saved

---

## Method 3: Search in Windows

### Steps:
1. **Press `Win + S`** to open Search
2. Type: **"Captures"**
3. Click on **"Captures"** folder
4. Your videos will be there

---

## Method 4: Direct Path

### Open Run Dialog:
1. **Press `Win + R`**
2. Type: `%USERPROFILE%\Videos\Captures`
3. Press **Enter**
4. Your videos folder opens!

---

## Video File Details:

- **Location:** `C:\Users\Akanksha\Videos\Captures`
- **Format:** MP4
- **Name:** `Recording_YYYY-MM-DD_HH-MM-SS.mp4`
- **Example:** `Recording_2025-11-08_15-30-45.mp4`

---

## Quick Access:

### Pin to Quick Access:
1. Go to `C:\Users\Akanksha\Videos\Captures`
2. Right-click folder → **"Pin to Quick access"**
3. Now it appears in File Explorer sidebar!

---

## Play the Video:

1. **Double-click** the video file
2. It will open in Windows Media Player or your default video player
3. Or right-click → **"Open with"** → Choose your preferred player

---

## If You Can't Find It:

### Check Settings:
1. **Press `Win + G`** → Click **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Go to **"Captures"** section
3. Check **"Save game clips and screenshots"** is ON
4. Check the **"Save location"** path

---
